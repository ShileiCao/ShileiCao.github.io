Shilei Cao | 曹诗磊

Hi, I'm Shilei Cao. I'm a second-year master student from the IGCS Lab of Sun Yat-sen University under the supervision of Prof. Zheng. I also spent some time at the HPGC research group of Tsinghua University as a visiting student, supervised by Prof. Fu. During my undergraduate studies, I was very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Peng.

My current research interests focus on enhancing the robustness and adaptability of machine learning models under distribution shifts. Specifically, my work spans Transfer Learning (including Test-Time Adaptation, Federated Learning, and Domain Generalization), as well as Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) for large models, with cross-disciplinary applications in Remote Sensing, Weather Foundation Models, and LLM Reasoning.

🔥 I will attend ICLR 2026 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil from April 23rd to 27th. Welcome to the conference site to communicate with me.
